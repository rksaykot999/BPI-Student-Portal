About BPI

Bogra Polytechnic Institute (BPI) is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical education. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their careers and contribute to a better society.

We offer a wide range of diploma programs, state-of-the-art laboratories, and experienced faculty to ensure a comprehensive learning experience.

Our Academic Programs

Computer Science

A robust program focused on software development, networking, and modern computing technologies.

Civil Engineering

Learn the fundamentals of designing and constructing buildings, bridges, and infrastructure.

Electrical Technology

Explore the world of electrical systems, power generation, and electronics with hands-on training.

Mechanical Technology

Master the principles of machinery, thermodynamics, and manufacturing processes.

Electronics Technology

Dive deep into the design, development, and application of electronic circuits and systems.
